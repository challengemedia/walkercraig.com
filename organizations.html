Challenge Media

Motto: "Challenge Everything."

Officially formed in the late summer of 2016, Challenge Media is a new media company made for a new age.
If you've been on the internet over the last 20 years, it's likely you've seen some of our work. What began as a loose collective posting art wherever there was a submit/post button, Challenge Media has now gone legitimate publishing creative content for the internet and its own digital publications.
Challenge Media is a unique startup that is uncompromising in its belief in editorial independence. The company refuses to cater to outside shareholder investment or the whims of venture capital allowing the business to remain committed to both its employees and its readership/subscriber base.

Evangelines

The Evangelines are a small group of practitioners of what can best be described as both a religious philosophy and a philosophy of religion. A metareligion and a metaphilosophy. Some call it a syncretism while others call it a return to the foundation of spirituality. It is ancient and it is modern. It may be the first of its kind, and it is perhaps the last of its kind. Do not be afraid.
